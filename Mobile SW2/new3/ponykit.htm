 Part list
   
  • PonyProg SI PCB v2.0 (double side with solder and silk screen)
    • Main board. Connect it to the serial port of the PC
    • I²CBus adapter board. Connect it to the Main board
    • Other E²PROM adapter board. Connect it to the Main board
    • AVR adapter board. Connect it to the Main board
    • PIC adapter board. Connect it to the Main board
  • Resistors:
    • 2 x 1K
    • 1 x 2K2
    • 6 x 4K7
    • 7 x 10K
    • 1 x 15K
  • Capacitors:
    • 2 x 47uF
    • 12 x 100nF
    • 2 x 27pF
  • Diodes:
    • 3 x 1N4148
    • 3 x Zener 5V1
    • 1 x Zener 13V
  • Other Semiconductors:
    • 4 x BC547 (NPN transistor)
    • 2 x BC557 (PNP transistor)
    • 1 x LM2936Z-5 (low dropout voltage regulator)
  • Xtal:
    • 1 x 4MHz
  • Connectors:
    • 1 x DB9 Female
    • 2 x AMP module 1x2p
    • 1 x AMP module 1x10p
    • 3 x Strip male SIL 3p (jumper)
    • 1 x Strip male SIL 2p (jumper)
  • Sockets:
    • 7 x DIL 8p
    • 1 x DIL 18p
    • 1 x DIL 20p
    • 1 x DIL 28p (300mils wide)
    • 1 x DIL 40p
