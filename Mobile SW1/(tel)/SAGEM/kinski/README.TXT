Manual for unlocking Sagem rc820.

On some old motherboards interface do not work properly without external power supply (about 9V).

- start sagemNCK.exe and select desired com port
- connect phone and turn it on.
- wait on about 10 second for phone init.
- read NCK
- disconnect phone and put simcard in it.
- start phone and enter readed NCK and press OK.
- the phone will be unlocked now ;)
