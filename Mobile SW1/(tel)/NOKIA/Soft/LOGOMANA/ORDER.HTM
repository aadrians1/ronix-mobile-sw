How to order LogoManager

You can order LogoManager from various different sources, using whichever payment method you prefer. When your order is processed, you will be sent a registration key via email (UK residents also have the option of receiving the full version via surface mail), which will unlock the demo copy. As a registered user, you may obtain free updates by downloading them from the web site.

The cost of registering the personal edition of LogoManager is EU17.95 (US $19.95, UK £9.95).
Note that companies who wish to use LogoManager must obtain a commercial licence, available for EU99 (US $99).
