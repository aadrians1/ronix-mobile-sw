
1. Run eric_old.exe or eric_new.exe (depending on your phone version)
2. Push the "NO" button and follow the instructions
3. Click the "Expert Menu" butten (right from the "NO")
4. Uncheck "Ask IMEI"
5. Check "AutoErasing FLASH"
6. Uncheck "Use Ini File Settings"
7. Click the SMALL "Unlock" button (down on the left)
8. Click "Flash Phone Phase 1"
9. Click "Flash Phone Phase 2"
10. When asked for a .bin file, choose "txxwest.bin"
11. Wait until flashing is done.

READY!!!!!
