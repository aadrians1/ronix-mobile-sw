SCENIX SEMICONDUCTOR LAUNCHES DESIGN SEMINAR SERIES

Santa Clara, Calif. - March 30, 1998 - Scenix Semiconductor, Inc. and its North American distribution partner, EBV Electronics, will conduct a series of joint technical training seminars on the Scenix SX Series high-performance 8-bit microcontroller (MCU) family. Running from May 4 through May 22, the series will cover 13 cities throughout the U.S. (see schedule).
The hands-on seminar sessions will introduce the SX Series microcontroller and will present the design methodology for incorporating SX products into embedded designs. The seminars will review the features of the SX chips that provide unequalled performance and flexibility, including 50 MIPS (million instructions per second) operation, on-chip EEPROM-based flash memory, deterministic interrupt handling and Virtual Peripherals, which are software implementations of peripheral functions.
After attending the seminar, designers will be able to incorporate SX Series microcontrollers into embedded applications immediately, achieving increased system flexibility, lower costs and improved time to market. The only prerequisites for the sessions are knowledge of embedded controller design and basic knowledge of PC and Windows(r) 95 operation.
Time and Cost
Each seminar is scheduled for one-half day. The price is $49 per attendee, and a coupon is provided for a $49 rebate on the purchase of an SX Key Development System.
Registration Information
For more information or to register for a seminar session, contact the EBV Electronics customer support hot line at 1-800-556-0225, or visit the Scenix or EBV Web site.
About Scenix Semiconductor
Scenix Semiconductor, Inc. is headquartered in Santa Clara, Calif. Founded in 1996, the company's mission is to deliver high-performance, cost-effective, easy-to-use single-chip solutions for embedded systems. Scenix announced the SX Series family of microcontrollers in August 1997. Recently, the company completed a $7.6 million round of venture financing. Additional information on Scenix and the SX Series products can be found on the Web, at http://www.scenix.com.
About EBV Electronics
EBV Electronics, Inc. is a specialist distributor within the Memec International Components Group, a $1 billion distributor with divisions in 22 countries. In the U.S., EBV has offices in Alabama, California, Colorado, Florida, Georgia, Massachusetts, Maryland, North Carolina, New Jersey, New York, Oregon, Pennsylvania, Texas and Utah. It also has international offices in Guadalajara, Mexico, and Toronto, Canada. As a highly technical distributor, EBV focuses upon five markets: communications, instrumentation, multimedia, contract manufacturing and industrial control. The EBV Web site is located at http://www.ebv-electronics.com.
